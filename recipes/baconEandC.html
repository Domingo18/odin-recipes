Bacon Egg and Cheese

Description

A recipe for a Bacon, egg and cheese sandwich

You will need:

  • Bacon
  • Choice of Cheese
  • Choice of bread
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Eggs
  1. Heat a drizzle of oil on pan with medium-high heat.
  2. Layer bacon strips onto the pan.
  3. Once bacon are fried to a semi crunchy state transfer to plate with a sheet of Paper Towel to wick off excess oil
  4. Add egg onto the pan. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. If you would like your bread of choice toasted do so now
  6. Flip the egg over onto the yoke and add your cheese.Cook for 10-15 seconds. This will retain some yoke.
  7. Cook for 10-15 seconds with the lid over the pan. This will retain some yoke.
  8. Finally plate your bread and add your egg, cheese , and bacon in that order.
