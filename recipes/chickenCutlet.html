Chicken Cutlet

Description:

A standard breaded boneless chicken breast meal.

  • Boneless chicken breast
  • Bread crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Slice quarter inch slices of your boneless chicken breast
  2. Season both sides of each slice with salt and pepper
  3. Crack two eggs into a mixing bowl
  4. Set aside your bread crumbs in a seperate mixing bowl
  5. Coat each slice first in the egg batter followed by the bread crumbs
  6. Finally fry in moderate drizzle of oil with 3 minutes each side
