Seasoned Beef

Description

A recipe for a pan of seasoned beef. Can be paired with corn or flour tortillas

You will need:

  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sofrito
  • Tomato paste
  1. Heat a drizzle of oil on pan with medium-high heat.
  2. Add beef and season with all the dry seasoning
  3. Break up the beef with a spatula and add in sofrito along with the Tomato paste
  4. Cook beef until browned
